Argentum Community Journal

- The title addresses the approach of the webpage: the community around the game. It will show the history and the news of the community and the different branches created during these years.

Site Purpose

This site will help people around the world get to know the game history, the community around it and all you can do to be a part of it. It will also show the latest news to know the status of different ongoing projects.

Scenarios

  • What is Argentum Online?
  • What is Argentum Community?
  • How can I play the game?
  • How can I be part of the community?
  • What is goin on today in the game/community?
